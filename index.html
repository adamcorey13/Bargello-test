Bargello Overview

This page is for Bargello's kit in Champions of the Continent. Octopath Zero info is coming soon!

Intro

Bargello is a 5★ Thief, who can be overclassed to a 6★. He can function as a DPS, shield breaker, or buffer/debuffer. As a DPS, he excels in doing single target damage, and he can increase the damage cap of your entire party simply by being a part of it. He can also restore SP and ultimate uses, and his A4 accessory boosts the damage cap of the holder even further!

Key Skills

  • Give the Signal: Raise Phys. Atk. and Elem. Atk. of Entire Front Row by 15% and raise dagger and wind damage by 15%. Grant Damage Limit Up (effect: 30000/turns: 2/3/4/5).
  • Debilitate: Lower Phys. Def. and Elem. Def. of Single Foe by 20%.
  • Preservante: Deal Phys. Dagger damage to Single Foe 2 times (potency: 55/70/85/120). At Max Boost, consume 100 SP each from All Allies to increase the potency with each ally affected, up to x5 (with 8 travelers).
  • Valore Style (Passive): Raise Phys Atk and Elem. Atk. of Entire Front Row by 15% and grant Damage Limit Up (effect: 20000) when a broken enemy is present.
  • Cuffs of the Family (A4 Accessory: Gain Dmg. Limit Up (effect: 100000). Grant All Allies automatic SP recovery (amount: 20) when a broken enemy is present.

Should you pull for Bargello?

Bargello is a great addition to any team, even in today's current meta. His A4 is a great accessory for any DPS you might want to use since it boosts their damage cap by 100k and regenerates SP for the party.

  • F2P: Pull and land one copy, and use the Awakening Shard exchange for the A4.
  • Dolphin: Pull for one copy at least, perhaps go for more if you have the rubies.
  • Whale: Go crazy and get all the copies you want!
